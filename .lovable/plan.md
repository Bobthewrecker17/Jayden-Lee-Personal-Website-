
# Personal Activity Showcase Webpage

A clean, minimal single-page website highlighting your clubs, volunteering, and Christian activities.

## Sections (top to bottom)

### 1. Hero / About Me
- Your name in large text with a subtitle (e.g. "High School Student | Leader | Volunteer")
- Circular profile photo placeholder
- Short bio paragraph about who you are

### 2. Activities Timeline
- Vertical timeline layout showing your activities organized by year
- Each entry has a title, date range, description, and a category tag (Clubs, Volunteering, Faith)
- Color-coded tags for each category

### 3. Awards & Achievements
- Card grid displaying honors, certifications, and recognitions
- Each card shows the award name, organization, and date

### 4. Contact & Social Links
- Footer section with icon links to email, Instagram, LinkedIn, or other socials
- Simple contact form or mailto link

## Design
- Clean white background with subtle gray accents
- Soft rounded cards and smooth scroll between sections
- Sticky top navigation bar with links to each section
- Fully responsive for mobile and desktop
- Gentle fade-in animations as you scroll down
